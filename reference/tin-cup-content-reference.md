## 1. SITE OVERVIEW & PURPOSE

### What is the Tin Cup Challenge?

The Tin Cup Challenge is an innovative annual charitable fundraising event held in Teton Valley, Idaho. It combines a community race/walk event with a unique matching donation system that benefits local nonprofits.

**Tagline:** "Inspiring Philanthropy"

**Key Descriptors:**
- "One of the most innovative charitable fundraisers in the nation"
- "One of the most successful charitable fundraisers"
- Operating since 2007/2008 ("Since '07")

### Mission Statement

> "The Community Foundation of Teton Valley works to elevate lives through the power of generosity by championing the nonprofit sector, empowering donors, promoting a culture of giving, and serving as a catalyst in addressing critical community needs."

### Impact & Statistics (2025)

- **Total Raised in 2025:** $3,881,656
- **Cumulative Total (Since 2008):** $30,862,434
- **Tin Cup Donors:** 1,623
- **Tin Cup Challengers:** 228
- **Challenger Friends:** 69
- **Participating Nonprofits:** 64
- **Match Percentage:** 52%
- **Community Reach:** One in three households in Teton Valley give through the Tin Cup Challenge

## 4. HOW IT WORKS

### Two Ways to Give

**1. Community Donors (Designated Giving)**
- Make single designated gifts
- Allocated to specific nonprofits
- Gifts are matched through the Challenger Fund

**2. Challengers & Friends of the Challenge (Undesignated Giving)**
- Give at levels of $1,500+ (Challengers)
- Donations are undesignated (not earmarked for specific nonprofits)
- Creates the matching pool for all community donations

### The Matching System

**How the Match Works:**
- Challenger donations create an undesignated matching fund
- Community donations to specific nonprofits receive a percentage match
- 2025 match rate: 52%
- This "makes local giving go even further"
- ALL participating nonprofits benefit from the match on their designated donations

**Challenger Impact:**
> "Challengers are a special group of individuals and businesses who donate to the Challenger Fund. The key to this fundraiser's success."

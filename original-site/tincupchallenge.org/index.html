    Community of Volunteers

    Volunteering is vital to strengthening communities by bringing people together to support one another and create lasting positive change. When individuals give their time and talents, they help meet immediate needs, build deeper connections, and enjoy a shared sense of purpose. Volunteers are crucial in driving local initiatives forward, from supporting youth and seniors to protecting natural spaces and uplifting nonprofits. Their efforts foster resilience, compassion, and unity—making our communities stronger, more inclusive, and better equipped to thrive.

    Nonprofit Volunteer Opportunities

    Since ’07

    Inspiring Philanthropy

    After participating in the Community Foundation of Jackson Hole’s Old Bill’s Fun Run as a neighboring community for many years, local nonprofit leaders gathered in 2007 to consider starting our own fundraising mechanism on the west side of the Tetons. The Community Foundation of Teton Valley was created as an affiliate of the Community Foundation of Jackson Hole and has been serving Teton Valley nonprofits ever since. The Tin Cup Challenge has raised millions since its incep­tion in 2007 and made phil­an­thropy a house­hold word in Teton Valley.

    Every­one is invit­ed to participate in the Tin Cup Challenge! Run with us, learn about local non­prof­its, engage with the community, donate, and cele­brate philanthropy. Qual­i­fy­ing non­prof­it orga­ni­za­tions in Teton Valley are invit­ed to par­tic­i­pate in the Tin Cup Challenge program and on Event Day.

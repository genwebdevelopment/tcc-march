Community Foundation of Teton Valley

Supporting 64 Local Nonprofits

The Tin Cup Challenge is one of the most innovative charitable fundraisers in the nation. One in three households in Teton Valley give through the Tin Cup Challenge—it's a true community effort that has raised over $30.8 million since 2008.

Qualifying nonprofit organizations in Teton Valley are invited to participate in the Tin Cup Challenge program and on Event Day. Through our unique matching system, donations are amplified by Challenger contributions, making every gift go further for our community.
